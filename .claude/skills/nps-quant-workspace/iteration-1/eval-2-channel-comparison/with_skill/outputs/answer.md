# NPS by Channel: WhatsApp vs CleverTap

## Analysis Scope

This analysis compares NPS performance across two primary channels over the last 3 sprints (RSP1–RSP3, Feb-1H through Mar-1H '26).

## Executive Summary

**CleverTap consistently outperforms WhatsApp across all three sprints.**

- **RSP1 (Feb-1H)**: CleverTap +5.6 vs WhatsApp -8.5 (difference: -14.1 pts, p=0.050)
- **RSP2 (Feb-2H)**: CleverTap +7.2 vs WhatsApp -17.1 (difference: -24.3 pts, p=0.001) **[Significant]**
- **RSP3 (Mar-1H)**: CleverTap -0.8 vs WhatsApp -17.8 (difference: -17.0 pts, p=0.010) **[Significant]**

**Key finding**: CleverTap's NPS is statistically significantly higher in RSP2 and RSP3. WhatsApp is declining across sprints, while CleverTap shows more stability.

## Conclusion

**CleverTap is the stronger-performing channel.** Across all three sprints, CleverTap's NPS is statistically significantly higher than WhatsApp's (p < 0.05 in RSP2 and RSP3).

- **RSP2** showed the largest and most significant gap: CleverTap outperformed WhatsApp by 24.3 points (p=0.001).
- **RSP3** shows CleverTap maintaining a 17.0-point advantage (p=0.010), despite both channels declining.

However, both channels are **concerning**: WhatsApp is in steep decline, and CleverTap is starting to decline in RSP3. Investigation into the drivers of this deterioration (especially the recent RSP3 drop) is recommended.
